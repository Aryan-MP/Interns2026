Virat Kohli

Introduction

Virat Kohli is one of the greatest cricketers in the history of Indian cricket. Known for his aggressive batting style, leadership skills, and unmatched consistency, he has inspired millions of fans around the world.

Early Life

Virat Kohli was born on 5 November 1988 in Delhi, India. From a very young age, he showed a strong interest in cricket. He joined the West Delhi Cricket Academy and trained under coach Rajkumar Sharma.

Cricket Career

Virat Kohli made his international debut for India in 2008. He rose to fame after leading India to victory in the 2008 ICC Under-19 World Cup. Over the years, he became one of the most reliable batsmen across all formats of the game.

Kohli has served as the captain of the Indian cricket team and led the team to many memorable victories. He is especially known for his performances in run chases and high-pressure matches.

Achievements

Virat Kohli has won numerous awards, including the ICC Cricketer of the Year and the prestigious Padma Shri and Khel Ratna awards. He holds several records for fastest centuries and most runs in international cricket.

Personal Life

Virat Kohli is married to Bollywood actress Anushka Sharma. Outside cricket, he is passionate about fitness, philanthropy, and promoting a healthy lifestyle.
